Hack2Skills Project – Team Code Smashers

Introduction

Today, we are addressing a critical issue—the decreasing population of indigenous cow breeds in India—and presenting our innovative solution.

Cows have been an integral part of our society for centuries. They contribute to agriculture, dairy production, and even traditional medicine. However, due to factors like urbanization, crossbreeding, and lack of awareness, many native breeds are on the verge of extinction. This decline not only affects farmers but also disrupts our ecological balance.

Our Solution

To tackle this issue, our team has developed a comprehensive platform that focuses on the 22 different indigenous cow breeds of India. Our solution includes:

  • Nearby Veterinary Hospitals: We mapped three hospitals in each state or city, ensuring easy access to medical care.
  • Dietary Guidance: Educating farmers about what cows should eat and avoid, improving their health and lifespan.
  • Awareness & Conservation: Highlighting the importance of cows in agriculture, dairy farming, and culture, encouraging sustainable breeding practices.

Impact and Vision

By implementing our solution, we aim to preserve native breeds, support farmers, and promote a sustainable future. Let’s work together to safeguard our cows and, in turn, our heritage.
